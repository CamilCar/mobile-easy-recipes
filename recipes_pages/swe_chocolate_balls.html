Swedish Chocolate Balls

With coconut flakes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups rolled oats
  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • Half a cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 2 tablespoons strong coffee
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. Mix 4 cups oats, 1 1/4 white sugar, and 1/2 cup cocoa together in a bowl. Add 1 cup butter and mix the ingredients together to make a thick dough.
  2. Mix in 2 tbsp coffee, 1 tsp vanilla, until thoroughly blended.
  3. Place 1/3 cup of coconut flakes in a small bowl. Pinch off small amounts of dough and roll between your hands to make small balls.
  4. Roll the balls in the coconut flakes. Balls are ready to eat, or may be refrigerated 2 hours to become firmer.
