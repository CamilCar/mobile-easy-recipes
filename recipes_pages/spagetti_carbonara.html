Creamy Pasta Carbonara

With Bacon and Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 100g bacon
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 large eggs
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g butter
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large saucepan.
  2. Chop 100g bacon, and finely grate 50g pecorino cheese with 50g parmesan and mix.
  3. Mix 3 eggs in a bowl and add black pepper.
  4. Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add 400g spaghetti and let simmer until pasta is done.
  5. Cut 2 garlic cloves in fine pieces.
  6. Fry the bacon with the garlic. Add 50g butter into a frying pan.
  7. When the pasta is done, add it to the frying pan with the bacon, keep the pasta water.
  8. Mix the cheese with the eggs. Add it to the frying pan, take it off the heat and mix well.
  9. Add several tablespoons of pasta water to the frying pan. Add salt after flavour.

Garnish:

Sprinkle extra cheese as topping when serving.
