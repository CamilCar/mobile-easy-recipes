Beef Stew

With Carrots and Potato

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 big carrots
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 850g beef

    • For garnish:

  • Picked leaves of thyme sprig

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 160C. Chop 2 celery sticks, 1 onion and 2 carrots. Add it to a pan with 5 bay leaves, 1 thyme sprig, 1 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp butter.
  2. Cook for 10 minutes, then stir in 2 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp tomato purée, 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce and 2 crumbled beef stock cubes.
  3. Stir in 600ml hot water and 850gr beef and let simmer.
  4. Cover the pan and put it in the oven for 2 hours and 30 minutes. Then take off the lid and put it back in the oven for another 30 minutes, or until the meat is really tender.

Garnish:

Garnish with the picked leaves of the remaining thyme sprig.
