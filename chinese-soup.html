Chinese Tomato Soup

With Chicken and Noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • 1 litre water
  • 2 cans chopped tomato
  • 2 chicken fillets
  • 2 pcs chicken broth
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 6 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp ketjap manis
  • 2 tbsp white whine vinegar
  • 1 tsp sambal
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch

    • For garnish:

  • 1 green onion

Instructions

  1. Heat up 1 lire of water with 2 chicken fillets, 2pcs chicken broth, 1 yellow onion and 2 cloves of garlic. Let it all simmer for 1-1.5 hour.
  2. Take out the ingredients out of the water, save the water. Cut the chicken fillets in small pieces.
  3. Add the 2 cans of mashed tomato to the water and heat it up again.
  4. Mix together 6 tbsp sugar, 4 tbsp kejap, 2 tbsp white wine vinager, 1 tsp sambal and 1 tsp ginger in a bowl. Then add this while stirring the soup.
  5. Mix 2 tbsp corn starch with 50ml cold water in a glass(important!cold water). Pour it into the soup while stirring.
  6. Add the noodles and let the soup simmer for another 20 minutes.
  7. Add the chicken to the soup when 15 minutes has passed.

Garnish:

Chop the green onion into fine slices and add it as topping when serving.
